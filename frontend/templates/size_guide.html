How to Measure

1

Waist

Measure around the narrowest part of your waist, usually just above the belly button.

2

Hip

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, keeping the tape horizontal.

3

Inseam (Length)

Measure from the crotch seam to the bottom of the leg along the inner seam.

4

Thigh

Measure around the fullest part of your thigh, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

5

Rise

Measure from the crotch seam to the top of the waistband at the front.
